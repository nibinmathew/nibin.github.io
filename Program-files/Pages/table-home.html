Table

In this page you're going to learn a new element.

Have you ever got a chance to see or touch on tabular information or tables?

A table is an arrangement of information in rows and columns. On webpages, a table will have a caption which is the title of the table and data within rows and columns represented vertically and horizontally respectively.

I will give you some important key commands to navigate through the table with your NVDA screen reader.

When you focus on the table, press and hold the Ctrl and Alt key together. Now you can use the Up, Down, Left and Right Arrow keys to navigate in different directions. Don’t release your fingers from Alt and Control keys unless you stop navigating through the table using arrow keys. Why don't you try these key commands on the tables below?
