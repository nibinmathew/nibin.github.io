Welcome to audio and video player.

In this page, we will explore a new element and I am sure you would love it the most when compared to other elements of the webpages. Below there is a media player which will play a video that you may enjoy. The media player will be provided with following elements and controls:

A caption for the video

A play/pause button

A seek slider to rewind and forward the video file.

A volume slider to increase and decrease the volume of the video file

A mute button.
