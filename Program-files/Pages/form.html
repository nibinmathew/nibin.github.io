Forms on web pages

You may be required to fill various forms on websites in future. The following activity will help you understand the structure of the form and how to fill them using NVDA. You may be already aware of the form control from the dialog window controls.

You can use the following keys to navigate through the page:

Tab key to navigate through the controls

Arrow key to navigate elements inside group controls.

Alt+Down arrow to expand the combo boxes.

Space key to activate buttons, checkbox and radio button.
